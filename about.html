About Zphisher

Zphisher is a powerful phishing tool used for social engineering research, security awareness, and testing. It provides a variety of well-designed phishing templates such as Instagram, Twitter, PayPal, and Snapchat to simulate real-world phishing scenarios.

This tool is built for educational and ethical hacking purposes only. It is intended to help security professionals understand and mitigate phishing attacks by demonstrating how easily users can be fooled by familiar user interfaces.

Please use this responsibly and only in environments where you have permission to test.
